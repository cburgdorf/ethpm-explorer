EthPM Registry Explorer

Hello, there.

This tool was built to help you explore the wild & wonderful package registries scattered across Ethereum's blockchains. But, be careful . . .

You CANNOT trust every package you see.

Just because a package is available, does NOT guarantee that it is safe to use.

To safely use a package, you must . . .

1. Independently verify the package's assets.

or

2. Trust the registry's owner to publish safe packages.
